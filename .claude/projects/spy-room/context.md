# Context

## What is Spy Room?
A social deduction party game where players pass a single device around. One player is the spy and must figure out the secret word, while other players try to identify the spy through questions and discussion.

## Game modes
- **Local (pass-and-play):** Single device, players take turns viewing their role.
- **Multiplayer:** Firebase-backed, each player on own device via room codes.

## Target users
- Friend groups at parties/gatherings
- Casual gamers who enjoy social deduction (Spyfall, Werewolf, Codenames)
- Both English and Lithuanian speakers

## Monetisation
- Free: 5 categories (Random, Everyday Objects, Famous People, Animals, Irish Slang)
- Premium: 6 additional categories via RevenueCat IAP (entitlement key: `'premium'`)
- Weekly voted categories as engagement feature

## Brand tone
- Fun, accessible, party-friendly
- Dark neon gaming aesthetic
- Not overly serious — it's a party game
